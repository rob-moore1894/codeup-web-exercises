FAQ about National Parks

How many National Parks are in the US?
There are 60 protected areas identified as National Parks

What is the first National Park in the US?
Yellowstone National Park became a US National Park in 1872 by President Ulysses S. Grant.

When was the National Park Service established?
On August 25, 1916, President Woodrow Wilson signed the act creating the National Park Service, a new federal bureau in the Department of the Interior responsible for protecting the 35 national parks and monuments then managed by the department and those yet to be established.
